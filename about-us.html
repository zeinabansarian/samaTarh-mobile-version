About Sama Tarh

Sama Tarh Manufacturing-Industrial Group founded table and chair production in Iran in 1353. With over 40 years of experience, their collection produces domestic goods that can compete with foreign products. They design, equip, and produce all kinds of furniture for various settings such as offices, hospitals, educational centers, and restaurants, ranging from office tables to amphitheater chairs. Sama Tarh's production-industrial complex offers high-quality products at the most affordable prices and has a wide variety of products seen in various settings throughout Iran.

who we are

Afshin Hojjat was born in 1353 in Tehran. He has a bachelor's degree in civil engineering and a master's degree in architecture. The design team of Afshin Hojjat and colleagues has presented special designs based on Iranian style since 1377 until now with the aim of develg urban architecture in traditional, modern, postmodern, neoclassical and hattic styles.

According to him, the task of an architect is to create a space in a way that, while crystallizing the category of beauty, people feel peace, security and well-being in that space.
